About Us

What we are

The propitious geographic location of the Republic of Turkey, between Europe, the Middle East and North Africa is one that provides enormous possibilities from a trade perspective. Being one of the most dynamic and stable emerging country on the planet, Turkey is destined to provide some of the most exciting growth opportunities for consumer goods manufacturers. In fact, Turkey is the 29th largest export economy and the 21th largest import economy in the world. Being amongst the world’s leading top producers of agricultural products, textiles, motor vehicles, transportation, equipment, construction materials, consumer electronics and home appliances, the country enjoys the 17th position in the world in terms of nominal GDP, and the 13th position in terms of GDP by PPP.

SIT-HUB Fairs and Organization is dedicated at the organization and coordination of trade fairs in Turkey by being an intersection point between foreign companies and Turkish companies. Our vision is to contribute at increasing the world’s total volume of trade through facilitating exports and imports in Turkey. Our mission is to bring together companies and organizations operating in the same fields to foster their volume of trade, and bridging the gap between international suppliers and international buyers. We provide international booth sales, international hosted buyer programs and consultancy services for international markets. Our role is to help complete package of sales, directing the companies in search of new markets to right destinations, and develop strategies for improving their share in the market. We also provide expertise, advice, strategies, translation services, as well as the coordination of advertising, exhibition and organizations of fairs in Turkey. The course of our work involves dealing with growers, exporters, importers, retailers, wholesalers, packaging and handling specialists, logistics professionals, together with the market chain representatives of from domestic and foreign markets, as well as stakeholders from public and private sectors.
